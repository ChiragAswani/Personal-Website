Best Restaurants in the Bay Area

By: Chirag Aswani Nov 19, 2019
Indian

Pakwan

Dhaba style restaurant with the best butter chicken and palak paneer.

  • 41068 Fremont Blvd
  • (510) 226-6234
Chinese

Ho Chow

Vegetarian friendly chinese food with. Best dishes include string beans and hot and sour soup

  • 47966 Warm Springs Blvd
  • (510) 657-0683
Mexican

Tacos Santiaguito

Small taco truck with shredded chicken. Ask for El Dorado style where they fry the tortilla before serving

  • 350 Saratoga Ave
  • (650) 669-2507
Pizza

Cicero's Pizza

Local pizza joint perfect for people who love thin crust, extra crispy pizza

  • 6138 Bollinger Rd
  • (408) 777-0690
Mediterranean

Achillies

5/5 rating on Yelp with 1,000+ reviews. That should say it all

  • 2521 Newhall St
  • (408) 984-2204
Burmese

Rangoon Ruby

Upscale burmese food. Try the chicken kebat and tea leaf salad

  • 445 Emerson St
  • (650) 323-6543
Sandwiches

Ike's Place

With over 300 different sandwiches and the special "dirty" sauce, Ike's is the best sandwich spot in the bay.

  • 75 E Santa Clara St Ste 130
  • (408) 294-2802
